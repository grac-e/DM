MAKING THE MOST OF DIGITAL

A digitally mature organisation is one that is making the best use of digital technology and its associated culture and networks in everything it does.

This means digital is part of the DNA of the organisation. Being digitally mature is different to digitising systems, processes, products and activities.

THE SIX-LEVEL ASSESSMENT METRIC

LEVEL PERCENTAGE RANGE IMPLICATIONS
0 0 to 15.5   Aspect practices are yet to be implemented or incomplete, Organization only performs essential operations.
1 16 to 34.5 Practices are fully implemented. Transition to Industry 4.0 has commenced.
2 35 to 50.5   Initial implementation of Industry 4.0 technologies. No integration yet. Physical systems can be represented virtually.
3 51 to 67.5   Vertical integration from shop floor to ERP level, Standardization of processes and operations.
4 68 to 84.5  Horizontal integration across the value chain. Application of Industry 4.0 technologies such Big Data and artificial intelligence. Autonomous optimization.
5 85 to 100  End-to-end integration. Continuous improvement. Smart and autonomous optimization.
